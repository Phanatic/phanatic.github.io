I dream about the night
I dream I am batman driving the batmobile , all black
I dream I am punching bad guys ,
Without having to hold back
I dream I have a credible disguise,
and on my arrival , bad guys act Surprised.
I dream I have a cool bat cave,
and all the money Wayne Senior gave .

                                     -Superman ( Clark Kent)
