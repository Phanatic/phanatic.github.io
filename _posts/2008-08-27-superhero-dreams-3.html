I dream I am Robin,
I am the only ray of color in Gotham’s dark nights,
I skip , I jump , I punch and prance,
like a graceful hippo’s dance.
I would jump off a rooftop,
Good God this is painful to write ,
I think I’ll stop.
    
                                       -Robin ( Dick Grayson )
